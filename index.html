About Me

Game Developer & Creative Coder

With over 7 years of experience in game development, I've had the pleasure of working on a diverse range of projects from mobile games to immersive VR experiences. My passion for creating interactive worlds began in childhood when I first discovered the magic of video games.

I specialize in gameplay programming, procedural generation, and game systems architecture. My approach combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving to build engaging player experiences that are both fun and technically sound.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring forest trails, studying game design theory, and contributing to game jams to keep my creative skills sharp.
